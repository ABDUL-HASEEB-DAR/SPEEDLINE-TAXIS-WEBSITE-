ABOUT US

Starting with only 1 car from our office in the TOWN of Dunstable in 2015. Our unique offering of a personal, high-quality service has revolutionized our industry and helped us to become the key player we are now. Our contracts with leading Hotels, Restaurants, Bars, Casinos, and Corporate Businesses are a testament to the priority we place on a level of professionalism previously unseen in the Private Hire industry. Long-standing accounts with companies such as Hilton, Home care, and City Council are a tribute to our success.

With bookings available online via eBooking, Email: enquiries@speedlinetaxis.org.uk as well as our 24 hour call center alongside a choice of payment methods such as account, cash, dockets and major credit or debit cards, booking with Speedline taxis Dunstable can easily be adapted to suit you.

OUR SERVICES

Airpot Transfer

Our Airport Transfer service presents a variety of vehicles tailored to match your preferences, ranging from sedans to SUVs. No matter your specific transportation requirements, we've got the perfect solution for you.

Baby Seat Services

Our Baby Seat Service provides an extensive range of car seats tailored to your child's age and weight. We ensure each car seat is correctly installed and securely fastened, offering you peace of mind.

Wheelchair Services
